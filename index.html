The record of Nürburgring

Posted on 05/15/2024

The hybrid hypercar took 6:35.183 minutes to travel through the Green Hell on October 28 at 5:14 p.m. The person in charge of completing the record lap was Maro Engel, a German driver who knows very well what it means to go flat out on the German circuit, since he won the 24 Hours of Nürburgring in 2016 with the help of another illustrious Mercedes driver: Bernd Schneider .

To achieve the record, Engel had to work hard and make the most of the electrical contribution of the hybrid propulsion system of the Mercedes-AMG One , derived from the German manufacturer's Formula 1.

And electrification has changed the way of driving a hypercar and it is no longer enough to know how to drive , we must also take into account energy recovery and how to use that extra help, without forgetting other AMG One solutions, such as the DRS, which is key on the straights and braking.



'Round' weekend for Verstappen: victory in Imola and victory in the virtual 24 Hours of Nürburgring

Posted on 05/19/2024




Max Verstappen is special... we all know that. He is also a true fan of racing and motorsports. But is it really that big of a deal? It is not strange to think so when the Red Bull driver has participated in the Formula 1 Emilia Romagna GP and the virtual 24 Hours of Nürburgring in the same weekend . And not only that, but he has won both tests! Truly, this guy's thing is something from another planet...

This weekend, in addition to the Formula 1 Emilia Romagna Grand Prix, the virtual Nürburgring 24 Hours took place. The team supported by Max Verstappen, Team Redline, was present at the test and the Dutchman, despite his commitments to F1 in Imola, participated in the race with his teammatesDiogo Pinto, Christopher Lulham and Florian Lebigre .

Aboard a M4 GT3 –specifically number 20 –, Max completed his first stint last night, after tacking Pole a few hours earlier at the Autodromo Enzo e Dino Ferrari.

In any case, the most curious thing is that it was he who placed the #20 car at the top of the table, since until then they were running in third position.

His second and final relay took place early this morning. Max Verstappenfinally taken the victory with 36 seconds of advantage over the second classified.

It seems unbelievable that this really happened. In any case, this is not the first time that the current Formula 1 world champion and the winner of the race in Imola this Sunday participates in a 'simracing' race at the same time that a Grand Prix of the category is being held. Queen of motorsports. It has already done so in Saudi Arabia and, in principle, the Hungarian event will be combined with the virtual 24 Hours of Spa-Francorchamps.
